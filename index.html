Notification

1 - type - type of notification, success, error, info, warning (default: success)

2 - title - text, title of notification (is required)

3 - mes - text, message of notification

4 - position - position, top-right, top-left, bottom-left, bottom-right (default: top-right)

5 - icon - string, icon of notification (default: due to type)

6 - time - notification duration in milliseconds (default: 4000)

7 - progress - true/false, to show progress line (default: true)
